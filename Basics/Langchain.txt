- What Langchain he is?
Langchain is a simple framework, that simplifies the development of LLM (Large Language Model) powered Applications.

- Langchain integrates you to call any LLM (i.e, GPT, GEMINI, LLAMA, MISTRAL), which powers GEN AI - by using that class that represents that particular Model.
